How to Use

  1. Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
  2. Allow camera access when prompted
  3. Position the barcode inside the scanner frame
  4. Scanner will automatically stop and play a sound when code is detected
  5. Click "Start Scanner" again to scan another code

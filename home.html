How to Scan Product Barcodes

  1. Click "Start Scanner" to activate your camera
  2. Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
  3. Position the barcode inside the scanner frame
  4. Ensure good lighting and hold the barcode steady
  5. The scanner will verify the barcode before confirming
  6. View the scanned barcode number and format
